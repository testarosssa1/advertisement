Overview

Content Type: Gaming (Walkthroughs, Reviews, Gameplay, Streaming)

Target Audience: Ages 13-30, predominantly male, global audience focused on North America, Europe, and Southeast Asia.

Psychographics

  • Interests: Video games, tech gadgets, esports, online communities
  • Values: Entertainment, authenticity, community
  • Lifestyle: Gaming as a primary hobby, connected to gaming culture

Goals & Pain Points

  • Goals: Improve gaming skills, discover new games, connect with relatable creators
  • Pain Points: Difficulty in progressing in games, finding trustworthy reviews

Content Preferences

  • Format: Short to mid-length videos, live streams, highlight reels
  • Style: Energetic, humorous, interactive, high-quality visuals

Viewing Habits

  • Platforms: YouTube, Twitch, Instagram/Twitter
  • Frequency: Daily viewers, often evenings/weekends
  • Engagement: Comments, likes, shares, especially during live streams

Key Takeaways

Focus on trending games, engage with audience, offer challenges, collaborate with other gamers.
